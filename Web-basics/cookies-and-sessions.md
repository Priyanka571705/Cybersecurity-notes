# Cookies and Sessions

## What are Cookies?
Cookies are small pieces of data
stored in the user's browser.

## Why Cookies are Used
- To store login information
- To remember user preferences

## What are Sessions?
Sessions store user data on the server
and use a session ID to track users.

## Cookie vs Session

| Cookies | Sessions |
|-------|---------|
| Stored in browser | Stored on server |
| Less secure | More secure |
| Client-side | Server-side |

## Simple Example
After login, a session keeps the user logged in
until logout.

## Simple Note
Sessions are safer than cookies for authentication.
